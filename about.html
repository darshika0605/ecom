About Us

LIFESTYLE STORE is a young's contemporary wholesale brand specializing in denim jeans. Established in 2018, The corporate office with its core design team is located in Bhopal Madhyapradesh, this allows us to access the newest denim trend.

Since the beginning of 2020, LIFESTYLE STORE has continually been creating and developing denim wear with equal emphasis on comfort, aesthetic, and quality. We pride ourselves in offering a trend right curated selection of jeans that fit perfectly. All our passion, dedication, and creative talents are given to each product that we design.

Our main goal is to provide the highest quality garment at the most reasonable price with the best customer service experience.
